Seasonal & Weather-Based - Variability

Solar generation varies over time; price signals on the day-ahead market create periods of high price and low supply — storage/buffers are needed to shift energy to these periods.

PV Produced Energy

Energy Prices on Day-Ahead Market

The Challenge

Three key challenges must be addressed for effective optimization and management:

Storage

Solved by batteries — enables energy to be stored during surplus periods and released when needed

Control

Solved by direct connectivity — real-time communication and command of distributed assets

Intelligence

Solved by context and data — smart algorithms leverage operational data to optimize energy flows
