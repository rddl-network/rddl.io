Proof-of-Productivity (PoP)

Hardware-anchored incentives for real-world energy productivity

1

2

Why it matters

  • Signed data minimizes oracle risk
  • Economic incentives tied to productivity

cRDDL Redemption

  • Redeem to release liquid RDDL
5 / 8

Supply & Distribution Logic

  • Initial valuation: RDDL at €0.04, establishing a 100:1 ratio to PLMNT (€0.0004).
  • Mining/Incentives: Majority of supply distributed to productive machines.
  • Liquidity: 10% of the initial DAO pool seeded into the Planetmint exchange.
  • Deflationary pressure: PLMNT can only be minted by locking/spending RDDL.
  • Adoption flywheel: More machines → more PLMNT demand → less RDDL in circulation.
